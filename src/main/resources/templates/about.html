About Me

Hi! I’m [Amit Kumar], the sole developer behind Expense Tracker. I built this app with the goal of helping people take control of their finances with an intuitive, reliable, and secure tool — because I know how important budgeting is in everyday life.

When I’m not coding or designing, I enjoy learning about personal finance and finding new ways to make budgeting easier and more accessible for everyone.

Why Expense Tracker?

Managing expenses can be overwhelming. That’s why I created this app to simplify tracking your spending habits, generate insightful reports, and keep your data safe and private.

Whether you're just starting to budget or looking for advanced insights, Expense Tracker adapts to your needs.
